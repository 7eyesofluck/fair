Fair Play Initiative

Fair Play is a player-led initiative pushing for honesty, accountability, and respect in gaming. The goal is to hold companies responsible for broken launches, exploitative monetization, and misleading marketing, while highlighting studios that treat players fairly. We aim to give players a voice.
